Abbreviations

Price checking and margins

INS (Instant Sell)

The price at which an item instantly sells for when listed in the ge. This is usually where you want to buy around.



INB (Instant Buy)

The price at which an item instantly buys for when listed in the ge. This is usually where you want to sell around.

NIS (Non Instant Sell

A sell offer that doesn't instant ly complete. This means your offer was the lowest sell offer at that time. Try and stretch the margin by upping the sell price
